Traditional Food

Nepali cuisine is known for its variety and richness. Some popular dishes include:

  • Dal Bhat - A traditional lentil soup served with rice and vegetables.
  • Momo - Dumplings filled with meat or vegetables, steamed or fried.
  • Sel Roti - A traditional Nepali sweet rice doughnut.
  • Chatamari - A type of rice crepe popular in the Newar community.

Culture

Nepal is rich in culture, with diverse traditions and festivals celebrated across the country. Key highlights include:

  • Tihar - Known as the festival of lights, celebrated with candles, rangoli, and worship of animals.
  • Holi - The festival of colors, celebrated with dancing and the throwing of colored powder.
  • Dashain - The biggest festival in Nepal, similar to Diwali, celebrating victory and the goddess Durga.
