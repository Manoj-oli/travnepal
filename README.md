# TravNepal 🏔️

A travel blog for tourists planning to visit Nepal — destinations, tips, and travel stories.

## About

TravNepal is a travel guide blog covering Nepal's top destinations, culture, and tips for first-time and experienced travelers. Whether you're planning to trek the Himalayas, explore Kathmandu's streets, or relax by Pokhara's lakes — this blog has something for you.

## Pages

- **Home** — Welcome page with featured destinations
- **Destinations** — Explore top places to visit in Nepal
- **Blog** — Travel articles and stories
- **Gallery** — Photos from across Nepal
